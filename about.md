Hi, I am searat \[siː-ræt\]. Whenever I’m making small talk with coworkers or vague acquaintances,
there’s an almost guaranteed moment where someone asks, “So… what do you do in your free time?”

I then pause, which I like to think gives off an air of intrigue,
but is actually me conducting a rapid scanning of several loosely managed pet projects.
None of them can be explained quickly, some of them shouldn’t be explained at all,
and a few even sound like I’ve made it up on the spot.

So I usually pick the least alarming option and attempt a tidy,
socially acceptable summary, just as the conversation quietly loses interest
and pivots to someone else who, reassuringly, "just goes to the gym."

And with that, my hobbies return to obscurity, where they belong.

But that ends now. I’ve finally started a blog, so I can confidently point people toward it and say,
"RTFB", which, for the sake of politeness, stands for Read the Blog, of course.
